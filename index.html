Hope Squad

Hope Squad is a mobile game that turns volunteering into fun! Players compete in capture-the-flag matches against AI-controlled teams formed by other players. To customize their team, they collect mascots, each representing a partner NGO with unique in-game abilities. To level up a mascot faster, players complete real-world missions that support the NGO, such as donations or social media promotion. As they collect and evolve mascots, players learn about these organizations, connect with their causes, and contribute to positive social impact.

Hope Squad is pioneer in encouraging NGOs in Rio de Janeiro and envolving the player in their causes. Hope Squad has parternship with 3 NGOs: Hemocione, Fazendo Arte and Guardiões do Mar.

Skills Developed: Unity game development, SpriteKit, In-App Purchases, SOLID, Social Business

Dimentionaut

Dimensionaut is a 3-minute playful experience with the main objective of instigating curiosity and the quest for knowledge about spatial dimensions. It is a small game where the player needs to navigate between dimensions to overcome obstacles. It was an intense experience: I had about a month to learn how to program in Swift - the main programming language for iOS development - come up with an idea, and develop the product. I am very grateful to the mentors at the Apple Developer Academy | PUC-Rio for providing me with all the necessary knowledge for this experience.

Dimentionaut is my playground winner of WWDC Swift Student Challenge 2024!

Skills Developed: SpriteKit, SceneKit
